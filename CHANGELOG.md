# Changelog

## Augustus 1.0.0 (2025-12-28)

### Features
- **Privacy & Terms**: Added Privacy Policy, Terms of Service pages, and registration checkbox.
- **Story Management**: New Stories page for creating, editing, and managing stories in batch.
- **Contact Linking**: Ability to link contacts to multiple stories, filter contacts by story, and manage links from profile.
- **Help & About**: New page explaining features and workflow.
- **Dashboard**: Full-width progress bar, improved layout, editable story titles/descriptions.
- **Notifications**: Functional notification bell (visuals updated).

### Fixes
- **Authentication**: Case-insensitive email login.
- **UI**: Fixed user avatar initials.
- **Cleanup**: Removed dummy data from Questions, Outreach, and Review pages.
